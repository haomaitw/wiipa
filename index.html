America Innovation Invention Exhibition

Inventors of all ages are welcome to a three-day event featuring an exhibition, competition, and award ceremony.

The World Invention Intellectual Property Associations would like to invite all inventors to participate in the 2025 America Innovation Invention Exhibition. It will be a three-day event for the exhibition from August 15 to 16, competition including the award ceremony. The World Invention Intellectual Property Associations organized this exhibition.

Be inspired by expert speakers in design, video, and more

A lot of people joined this year's 2025 America Innovation Invention Exhibition especially students, companies and resource persons. This is because the exhibition has no age limit and is open to professionals as well. They all come here to communicate with other inventors and companies.
